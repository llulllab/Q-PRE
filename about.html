Mission

Q-PRE (Q Reports – Peer-Reviewed Edition: Journal of Combinatorial Practices) is dedicated to publishing innovative research at the intersection of mathematics, computation, culture, and the arts. Our mission is to provide a rigorous yet experimental medium for ideas that take multiple forms.

Scope

The journal welcomes contributions spanning theoretical models, experimental frameworks, cultural applications, and interdisciplinary approaches that combine analytical precision with creative exploration.
